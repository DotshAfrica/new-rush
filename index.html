Features.

We recognize that customer’s best understand their problems. Rush is tailored to enable customers to easily create their applications by providing a platform they can easily manipulate.

Inventory and Order management

Provides management and visibility of stock including stock levels, orders, stock movement in multiple distribution or collection points.

Task Allocation and monitoring

Allows management of activities conducted on the field like easy creation, editing, allocation, monitoring & approval of tasks as per the organization’s processes.

Reports and Analytics

A customizable analytics and reporting module that provides dashboards such as: performance management, stock availability, trends, etc.

Route optimization

Provides custom and optimized routes with navigation to field officers and delivery teams that saves on time, improves productivity, and ensures efficiency.

Modular

Pay for what you use. Easily incorporate the most innovative software solutions in your applications.

Team Collaboration

Rush provides an in-app collaboration module that helps teams collaborate via messaging, voice & video, and selected email API to messaging.
